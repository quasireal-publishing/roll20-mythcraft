CATAPULT

Catapults are heavy contraptions that can launch their payloads at high speeds and arching trajectories. While not as powerful as trebuchets, catapults are more maneuverable and can be used to launch specialty ammunition over a fortified wall. Beasts that can carry five or more unwieldy items can drag a catapult if it is mounted on wheels. A team of beasts that can collectively carry that many unwieldy items can also move the catapult. Catapults cost 5,000 sc.

Range: 1,000 ft; can clear 140 ft elevation

Ammunition: Ball (2 AP)

Reload: 4 AP

AOE: 10 ft radius

Heft: 12

Speed: 30 ft

HP 60

AR 13 REF 7 FORT 15

Resist Blunt 5 Immune Psychic

Vulnerable Fire 1

DR 5/Fire DT 8

A creature within 5 ft of the Catapult can spend 2 AP to make a siege weapon attack vs the FORT of each target in the radius. This attack suffers a -2 penalty against Medium or smaller targets. Hit: 4d10 blunt and the target falls Prone. Miss: damage and the target does not fall Prone. If the ball did not impact a surface (such as targeting a flying creature), it does not use its AOE radius and does no damage on a miss.

SIEGE TOWER

Siege towers are enormous, wheeled towers usually made of wood and reinforced with iron. They provide total cover to creatures within the tower and have drawbridges that can be lowered to allow soldiers to run from the top of the siege tower directly onto an enemys battlements. Beasts that can carry seven or more unwieldy items can drag or push a siege tower. A team of beasts that can collectively carry that many unwieldy items can also move the tower. Siege towers cost 8,000 sc.

Heft: 20

Speed: 20 ft

HP 200

AR 14 REF 5 FORT 16

Resist Blunt 6 Immune Psychic

Vulnerable Fire 1

DR 2/Fire DT 15

A creature in the top floor of the siege tower may spend 3 AP to cut the ropes, causing the drawbridge to rapidly lower.

TREBUCHET

A much larger and more powerful cousin to the catapult, a trebuchet is dozens of feet tall and can hurl stone balls almost as far as a cannon. Beasts that can carry seven or more unwieldy items can drag a trebuchet if it is mounted on wheels. A team of beasts that can collectively carry that many unwieldy items can also move the trebuchet. Trebuchets cost 9,000 sc.

Range: 2,300 ft; can clear 100 ft elevation

Ammunition: Ball (3 AP)

Reload: 4 AP

AOE: 20 ft radius

Heft: 20

Speed: 20 ft

HP 100

Vulnerable Fire 1

Hit: 6d10 blunt and the target falls Prone. Miss: damage and the target does not fall Prone.
