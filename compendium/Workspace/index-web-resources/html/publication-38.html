CHAPTER 7 | THE HOARD 

BRACERS OF CONDUCTION

Gloves, Rare, Essence 20

These silver gauntlets are decorated with lightning bolts of white crystal. You gain Resist Lightning 3. Additionally, when you take lightning damage, you gain 1 charge for every 3 damage taken, to a maximum of 4 charges. When you make a melee or ranged attack, you may spend +1 AP to consume any number of charges as part of the attack. If the attack hits, the target takes +1d10 lightning damage for every charge consumed.

BREWER’S CASK

Container, Common, Essence 10

This small oaken cask has two silver bands carved with images of grape vines. Any mundane item or liquid sealed inside the Brewer’s Cask ages 1 year for every hour.

CARRION CLOAK

Cloak, Rare, Essence 30

This cloak appears to be nothing more than tattered, foul-smelling rags infested with all manner of insects. The cloak has 1 charge, and regains its charge daily at dusk. You may spend 2 AP and 1 charge to summon 1d4 Swarms of Insects. These swarms appear in unoccupied spaces within 5 ft of you and go immediately after you in Initiative. They follow your mental commands and last for 10 minutes or until reduced to 0 HP, whereupon they dissipate.

CIRCLET OF MIND SHIELDING

Helmet, Rare, Essence 30

This plain steel circlet has a single ruby set on the forehead. You gain Immune Psychic, Charmed, and Enthralled. You are also Immune to any magical ability that reads thoughts.

CIRCLET OF THOUGHT DETECTION

Helmet, Rare, Essence 20

This elegant silver circlet is affixed with three purple gems over the temples and forehead. This circlet has 3 charges and regains 1 charge at dusk each night. You may spend 1 charge and 2 AP to read the thoughts of one creature you can see within 200 ft of you for 1 minute. These thoughts are always surface level, and the target does not become aware their thoughts are being read.

If you spend 1 minute concentrating on the circlet, taking 1d8 damage after this time, you may regain 1 charge. (1/Recoup)

CLIMBER’S GLOVES

Gloves, Uncommon, Essence 15

These sheepskin gloves have small metal protrusions on the tips of each finger. Gain a climb speed equal to your walking speed.

CLOAK OF SHADOWS

Cloak, Rare, Essence 20

This ragged linen cloak is dyed the deepest, darkest black. You gain +6 to DEX/Sneaking checks to remain hidden while in darkness. This cloak has 3 charges and regains all spent charges at dusk. When you spend AP to move, you can expend a charge to step through a shadow and reappear in an unoccupied space in another shadow you can see within 90 ft of you.

CRANE PAPER

Supernatural Item, Common, Essence 10

These pages of thin silver-edged paper are of an expert quality. After whispering a target’s name, the paper immediately folds into the shape of a bird and attempts to fly straight to the target’s location. If the target is not on the same plane of existence as the Crane Paper when activated, the paper immediately combusts.

DEPLOYABLE GLIDER

Container, Uncommon, Essence 15

This leather pack is fixed over a metal harness that can be worn by a small or medium sized creature. While worn, it functions as a normal traveler’s backpack or can be activated via a pull-string to deploy a pair of mechanical leather wings. For 10 minutes upon activation, you do not take falling damage, and for every 10 ft that you fall, you may move 10 ft horizontally. Once used, you must Take a Rest in order to reset this feature. (1/Rest)

ENDLESS QUIVER

Supernatural Item, Rare, Essence 25

This unassuming leather quiver is inlaid with bronze and appears empty. As part of a ranged attack, you can choose to magically produce and draw a bolt or arrow for use as ammunition. This bolt or arrow adds +1 to the attack and damage roll and disappears after striking a target. Only one bolt or arrow can be drawn from the Endless Quiver at any time.

FLUTE OF THE GRAVECALLER

Instrument, Epic, Essence 35

When played, this carved bone flute emits a haunting, discordant tone. You may spend 4 AP to play the flute and summon a Ghost to an unoccupied space within 20 ft of you. This ghost has the same skills and memories it did in life and must obey your commands. It takes its Initiative immediately after you and lasts for 1 minute or until reduced to 0 HP. The Ghost must answer your questions but does not have to answer honestly. Once this feature is activated, it cannot be used again for 48 hours.
