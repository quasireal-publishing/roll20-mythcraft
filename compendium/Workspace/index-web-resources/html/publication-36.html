MARINER’S FRIEND

DEX Weapon, One-Handed, Light, Common, Essence 10

Range: Melee

APC: 2

Damage: 1d6 sharp

This plain cutlass has a compass fixed into the hilt. You gain +4 to Navigating* checks and can always find true north. You cannot be unwillingly disarmed of this weapon.

MUL’ATOR BLADE

DEX Weapon, One-Handed, Concealed, Light, Rare, Essence 20

Range: Thrown 20 ft

APC: 2

Damage: 1d4+1 sharp

These curved blades are specially forged for the Mul’Ator assassins, who pride themselves on a single killing strike. Attacks with this weapon gain +1 to their crit range, and when you crit, after you calculate your damage, double it.

REALMSTALKER

DEX Weapon, Two-Handed, Unwieldy, Rare, Essence 35

Range: Melee

APC: 4-DEX (min 2)

Damage: 2d4+2 sharp

This black-handled scythe has a thin blade made of light blue crystal. This weapon has 2 charges and regains 1 charge at dusk each night. You may spend 1 charge and 6 AP to open a portal in the air. This portal may go to any location you have visited before so long as that location is on the same plane of existence. Any creature that steps through the portal as part of a movement action is instantly transported to the chosen location. After one minute, the portal closes.

SCORPION’S STING

DEX Weapon, One-Handed, Concealed, Light, Uncommon, Essence 15

Range: Thrown 20 ft

APC: 2

Damage: 1d4 sharp

This dagger appears to be the curved stinger of a giant scorpion with a crude, leather-wrapped hilt. When you hit, make a secondary attack with an additional +2 bonus against the target’s FORT. On a hit, 1d6 toxic damage.

SICKLE OF CULLING

DEX Weapon, One-Handed, Light, Uncommon, Essence 10

Range: Melee

APC: 2

Damage: 1d4+1 sharp

The handle of this sickle appears to be made of tightly twisted vines. Attacks made with this weapon against creatures with the plant tag gain +2 TA on their attack rolls and +2 on their damage rolls.

SOULBOUND DAGGER

DEX Weapon, One-Handed, Concealed, Light, Common, Essence 10

Range: Thrown 20 ft

APC: 2

Damage: 1d4 sharp

This unassuming iron dagger is well balanced for throwing. Immediately after using this dagger to make a ranged attack, it magically returns to your hand. You cannot be forcibly disarmed of this dagger.

SPIRIT BOW

DEX Weapon, Two-Handed, Rare, Essence 20

Range: Ranged 600 ft

APC: 6-STR (min 2)

Damage: 1d10+2 psychic

This weapon appears to be a plain black-leather arm guard commonly worn by archers. While worn, if both hands are free, you may make a ranged attack as if you were wielding a longbow. A translucent, ghostly image of this longbow appears in your hands along with an arrow to be used as ammunition. After the attack action is complete, both the weapon and the arrow immediately vanish.

STAFF OF VIPERS

DEX Weapon, Hand-and-a-Half (APC 2), Rare, Essence 20

Range: Melee

APC: 3

Damage: 1d4 blunt and 1d4 corrosive

This wooden quarterstaff appears to be completely wrapped in snake skin.

You may spend 4 AP to throw the staff onto the ground and summon a Constrictor Snake to an unoccupied space within 5 ft of you. This constrictor snake remains for 10 minutes, or until defeated, obeys your mental commands, and takes its turn immediately after you. (1/Rest)

STAFF OF WHIRLWINDS

DEX Weapon, Hand-and-a-Half (APC 2), Rare, Essence 20

APC: 3

Damage: 1d4+2 blunt

This white ash quarterstaff is wrapped in dark blue leather and capped in silver. The staff has 3 charges, and regains 1d3 charges daily at dawn. When you make a successful melee attack against a target that is Medium or smaller, that target is pushed back 5 ft away from you. Additionally, you may spend 4 AP and 1 charge to create a 20 ft cone of wind and make a special attack. Make an END attack against the FORT of all creatures in the cone. On a hit, deal 1d8+2 blunt damage and targets are pushed back 20 ft. On a miss, deal ½ damage and targets are not pushed away.

VAMPIRE’S BANE

STR Weapon, Hand-and-a-Half (APC 2), Uncommon, Essence 15

Range: Thrown 20 ft

APC: 3

Damage: 1d8+1 sharp

This sharpened white oak spear is wrapped in dark red leather. You gain Resist Charmed 5. When used to attack creatures with the undead tag, this weapon gains +2 to hit and deals +1d8 radiant damage. On a crit against undead, after calculating the critical damage, double it.

WAR WIZARD’S WAND

CHA Weapon, One-Handed, Rare, Essence 20

Range: Ranged 80 ft

APC: 4-CHA (min 2)

Damage: 1d8+2 fire, lightning, cold, or corrosive

This white ash wand is affixed with a 4-sided tumbler in the hilt. Each side has a single symbol: a flame (fire), a lightning bolt (lightning), a snowflake (cold), and a skull (corrosive). You may spend 1 AP to shift the tumbler to a specific symbol. Doing so changes the elemental damage type of all attacks made with the War Wizard’s Wand until changed again.

WASP

DEX Weapon, One-Handed, Uncommon, Essence 10

Range: Melee

APC: 5-DEX (min 2)

Damage: 1d8+1 sharp

The hilt of this iron rapier is wrapped in alternating yellow and black dyed leather. Each subsequent successful attack made with this weapon against the same target deals +1 sharp damage. This bonus resets at the start of each of your turns.

WOODCUTTER’S AXE

STR Weapon, Hand-and-a-Half (1d10), Common, Essence 10

Range: Melee

APC: 5-STR (min 3)

Damage: 1d8 sharp

This battleaxe has gnarled roots grown around its handle that weave up around the head of the axe. This weapon has 1 charge, which it regains daily at dawn. You can listen closely and hear soft whispers coming from the roots. You cannot be disarmed of this weapon. You may expend the weapon’s charge to discern the axe’s whispers: You find the location of nearby fresh water and enough food for 2d4 individuals, provided that water and food can be found within 1 mile.

CHAMELEON HIDE

This suit of specialized mail covers a medium humanoid’s entire body. It has 1 charge, which it regains 1 hour before dawn each day. You can activate the Diving Suit by spending the charge. You gain a swim speed equal to your walking speed and gain the ability to breathe underwater for 1 hour.

FLAMEWALKER’S ARMOR

Armor, Medium, Uncommon, Essence 15

Base: Scale Mail

The dark red scales of this scale mail armor appear to be made of chitin. You gain Immune Fire.
