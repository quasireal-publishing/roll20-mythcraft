WEST GATHANDIA TRADING COMPANY

The West Gathandia Trading Company, or WGC, is a private corporation that builds enormous deep-sea vessels in the shipyards of Pash. The WGC has warehouses and administration buildings in virtually every port city along the south and east of Gathandia, and has many established trading posts on the west coast and in the Inner Sea, as well. The WGC is currently developing vessels that should be capable of long-term voyages, allowing their crew to explore beyond Gathandia and make contact with the other continents.

Most national governments are not fond of WGC because of the company’s aggressive and monopolistic trade practices, but the wealth that they bring to the port cities is usually enough to make monarchs and other rulers begrudgingly overlook WGC’s less savory practices.

PERFORMING TROUPES

THE BUMBLEFUMBLE FESTIVAL

Hundreds of nomads travel together to put on the Bumblefumble Festival outside different cities across the continent. This festival includes carnival food and games, along with performances by the Bumblefumble bards and acrobats. Fire eaters, sword jugglers, lion tamers, and other eccentric performers also draw crowds in droves. The Bumblefumble Festival has a dark secret, however. All of their games are shams designed to separate participants from their coins while giving them just enough hope that they might win if they participate again. Most folk that work the carnival are also cunning pickpockets, lifting just a few coins at a time from their unsuspecting victims, but never stealing enough at once to raise undue suspicion. The entire carnival, which is the size of a small town when set up, can be broken down overnight, allowing the group to quickly clear out when they have overstayed their welcome. The Festival is overseen by conjoined twin halflings Bumble and Fumble, who are both skilled charlatans and can cast arcane magic, respectively specializing in enchanting and illusory functions.

CIRQUE DU PSION

A renowned and spectacular performing group that travels throughout Gathandia, especially in its southern nations, Cirque du Psion astounds commoner and noble alike.

Highly skilled acrobats enhance their performances with psionic powers, enabling them to leap to stunning heights, levitate during their somersaults, and telekinetically manipulate their silk ribbons and sparkling hoops to achieve beautiful synchronized dances. These performances are highly acclaimed and they usually fill a stadium to overflowing for several nights in a row when they perform in a highly populated town.

HARMONIEM

Nobles and other folk from high society can often be found attending performances put on by Harmoniem. This troupe of thirty talented orchestral performers travels Gathandia, performing in prestigious halls, at royal palaces, and in other highbrow locations. There are many such orchestras, but Harmoniem is by far and away the most famous. Harmoniem consists not only of the musical performers, but also skilled caravanners and security who accompany the musicians and protect them on the road.

THE MERRY MINSTRELS

The Merry Minstrels are a large, disorganized group of bards and musicians. They often travel in groups, but some strike out on their own. Whatever the case, the Merry Minstrels bring joy and cheer to the inns and taverns whence they perform. They eagerly seek out news songs and stories to add to their repertoire, informally spreading news, history, and folklore across Gathandia. Joining the Merry Minstrels is not a particularly formal process; one must perform a handful of songs or poetic recitations to a minimum of two current members of the Merry Minstrels. The most famous Merry Minstrel alive today is a six-fingered lute player named Daiquiri Fillingsworth.

STRAVONITES

Taking their name after the Stravon Conclave, the Stravonites are a traveling group of performers who put on elaborate plays. They are led by their dragonfolk playwright named Shilliam, and they have achieved international fame for their lowbrow humor and heartfelt acting. They perform most frequently in the Thunderhead Vale but have been known to travel as far west as Pash and as far northeast as Forsten.

SCHOLARLY ORDERS

ESSENCARY EPISTEMOLOGISTS

The Essencary Epistemologists are a group of scholars that travel around Gathandia giving lectures and debating with students and other scholars at any educational institution where they can find willing participants.

They can be identified by blue academic regalia with silver embroidered threading. By nature, these scholars are most interested in one’s state of being, of existing, and contemplating how the process of thinking works. They are especially interested in how Essence works, what it is, and how it affects a person’s capacity for thought and for experiencing the world. Essencary Epistemologists can be found in virtually any major university across Gathandia.

EVIDENTIARY ANALYSTS OF SCIENTIFIC PRINCIPLES

Physicists, chemists, biologists, and other scientists compose the academic guild known as the Evidentiary Analysts of Scientific Principles. They pursue both mundane and magical experimentation with alacrity, eager to better understand the workings of the physical and metaphysical. Some members of this scholarly order are themselves mages, while others are simply skilled scientists. Recognizable in their deep green academic regalia with red embroidery, these analysts can be found at any major university and some smaller academic institutions, as well. Scholars within this scientific order who specialize in astronomy and metaphysics have strenuous but constructive disagreements about the grand design of the multiverse.

ORDER OF DISCERNING PREWRITTEN HISTORY

In their light tan regalia with dark red embroidery, members of the Order of Discerning Prewritten History can be found both in the lecture hall and at archaeological sites. Scholars from this order are particularly interested in discovering and preserving anything that they can discern about ancient history, from the times before anyone began to record historical events in physical form. A mix of academic scholars, painstakingly careful archaeologists, and daring tomb raiders, members of the Order network with one another to broaden their understanding of ancient events.

The Order also corresponds regularly with the Historians Guild.

TUTORS EXTRAORDINAIRE

A nomadic and decentralized order of oracles, the Tutors Extraordinaire travel throughout Gathandia. They are highly sought after in any nation’s top universities. When a Tutor arrives in a university, they will offer classes and hands-on practice of their craft for any amount of time from a single day to multiple years. This generally depends on how beneficial a Tutor thinks that they can be to a school, but sometimes depends on the Tutor’s own whims and desires.

To become a Tutor Extraordinaire and earn the hooded sash that accompanies the rank, one must instruct classes for three years, overseen by a different Tutor Extraordinaire each year. After being approved by each of the three overseers, the teacher will be granted their sash.

WITCH COVENS

COVEN OF THE EVERWILDS

The Coven of the Everwilds is primarily located in its namesake haunt. Throughout the Everwilds, witches of this coven make their homes in villages or in the wilderness along with at least two of their fellow witches. Witches from this coven actively pursue positive relationships with the Archfae of all six Conclaves and are generally respected by fae of all types. Some cells of this coven can be found in Gathandia, especially in heavily forested areas such as the Kozia Rainforest and the nations around the northern Inner Sea. While these witches are seen as eccentric and at times creepy, many folk understand that their role as intermediaries between the mortal plane and the Archfae Conclaves is very valuable.

SYMPATHY COVEN

The Sympathy Coven is a secretive group of witches that rivals the Illuminati in influence and reach. The Sympathy Coven has far fewer witches than the Everwilds or Naturalist Covens, but these witches are cunning and powerful. Skilled in the art of creating poppets and subtly influencing the people represented by these poppets, the Sympathy Coven’s tentacles subtly pull the strings of power across Ancerra. Some members of the Sympathy Coven have designs on controlling whole empires or continents themselves, but the more altruistic members of the coven simply want to use their invisible hand to guide Gathandia’s nations away from violent conflict with one another. The Sympathy Coven often conducts acts of espionage against the Illuminati.

COVEN OF NATURALISTS

Of the three major witch covens on Gathandia, the Coven of Naturalists is the least consequential to world events. The Coven of Naturalists consists of witches who admire nature, especially its fauna. Many cells of this coven travel the world together, documenting and befriending the various beasts and monstrosities that they encounter. Others remain in roughly the same region for their whole lives and are beloved by the mundane and magical beasts of the region. A young halfling named Katsumash is believed to have the most potential to become a legendary Naturalist; although he has only been a member of the coven for a year, he has already caught the eye of several senior witches in the coven as having a particular way with animals.
