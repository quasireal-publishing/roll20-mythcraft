WHAT DOES IT DO?

If you don’t already have a clear idea of what you want an item to do, it can be hard to come up with inspiration. A few easy places to look are class talents, magic from any of the five Magic Sources, and monster abilities. Taking any of these and turning them into a magic item can give your players a fun and unique experience.

MAGIC ITEM CODEX

Items in this codex are organized first by weapons, then armor, then general items, and finally potions. Within those categories, they are presented alphabetically.

WEAPONS

ABYSSAL BLADE

STR Weapon, Two-Handed, Unwieldy, Rare, Essence 25

Range: Melee

APC: 8-STR (min 4)

Damage: 2d8+2 sharp

This steel greataxe has an oak handle capped in gold and it emits a faint glow. It has 1 charge, which it regains at midnight.

When you hit a creature, you may expend a charge. All protective spells affecting that target are immediately dispelled.

BASILISK’S FANG

DEX Weapon, One-Handed, Concealed, Light, Rare, Essence 30

Range: Thrown 20 ft

APC: 2

Damage: 1d4+2 sharp

The curved silver blade of this bone-hilted dagger is printed with three monstrous eyelids. This weapon has 3 charges and regains one charge every 7 days. You can spend 1 charge and 3 AP to make a special attack. Make a DEX attack against a living target’s WILL. On a hit, the target is Paralyzed. Creatures paralyzed by the Basilisk’s Fang may make a DC 15 END check at the start of each of their turns for the next 10 minutes to end this condition. If they do not succeed during this time, the effects become permanent until they are restored via Greater Restoration or similar magic.

BLADE OF THE Rodir

DEX Weapon, Hand-and-a-Half (1d10), Uncommon, Essence 25

Range: Melee

APC: 5-DEX (min 3)

Damage: 1d8+1 sharp

This folded steel katana has a hilt wrapped in red and blue dyed leather. You may spend 2 Reactive AP to make a DEX check when you are hit by a melee attack. If your roll is higher than the attack roll, that attack’s damage is reduced to 0 (you still suffer any other effects). You may only use this feature once per turn.

BLOODY BLADE

DEX Weapon, Hand-and-a-Half (1d10), Rare, Essence 25

Range: Melee

APC: 5-DEX (min 3)

Damage: 1d8+4 sharp

This ebony bladed katana has a hilt wrapped in blood-stained white cloth. When you hit with this weapon, regain HP equal to ½ the damage you dealt. However, while this weapon is equipped, you cannot regain hit points in any other manner. If you are Bloodied, you enter a bloody rage for your next 3 turns and must attack the closest creature to you. Attacks made while enraged in this way deal double damage.

BOOM-ERANG

DEX Weapon, One-Handed, Light, Rare, Essence 20

Range: Thrown 60 ft

APC: 2

Damage: 1d6 blunt and 1d6 sonic

This wooden boomerang is shaped like a bent lightning bolt. On a hit, the creature is Dazed and Deafened until the end of its next turn.

BOW OF STORMS

DEX Weapon, Two-Handed, Ammunition, Rare, Essence 20

Range: Ranged 600 ft

APC: 6-STR (min 2)

Damage: 1d10+2 sharp

Faintly glowing blue runes cover this white ash longbow. The Bow of Storms has 3 charges and regains all charges at dawn. You may spend 1 charge to speak a command word and convert the next fired arrow into a lightning bolt. Make a DEX attack against the target’s REF and the REF of each creature in a line between you and the target. Hit: 3d10 lightning damage. Miss: ½ damage.

BOW OF SHOOTING STARS

DEX Weapon, Two-Handed, Ammunition, Rare, Essence 15

Range: Ranged 600 ft

APC: 6-CHA (min 2)

Damage: 1d10+2 sharp

This longbow appears to be made from purple crystal. This bow has 3 charges and regains 1 charge at midnight. You may spend 1 charge when you hit to deal +1d6 radiant damage, and the target is Blinded until the end of their next turn.

CESTUS OF KNOCKING

STR Weapon, One-Handed, Light, Uncommon, Essence 15

Range: Melee

APC: 2-STR (min 1)

Damage: 1d4+1 blunt

This brass cestus has a large door knocker mounted over the knuckles.

You may spend 5 AP to strike the surface of a wall or floor. If the surface is made of wood or stone, the attack creates a hole 3 ft wide, 4 ft tall, and 1 ft deep. (1/Rest)

COLOSSUS GREATSLAB

STR Weapon, Two-Handed, Unwieldy, Uncommon, Essence 25

Range: Melee

APC: 8-STR (min 3)

Damage: 2d10+2 sharp

The iron blade of this greatsword is 3 ft wide and crudely made. You may spend 2 AP to plant the tip of the Colossus Greatslab into the ground. While planted, other creatures may only remove the Colossus Greatslab if they succeed on a DC 30 STR check, and the space the Colossus Greatslab occupies functions as a tower shield. While planted, this weapon cannot be used for abilities or to make attacks. Spending 2 AP allows you to pull the Colossus Greatslab from the ground, ending this feature.

COMMANDER’S LANCE

STR Weapon, Two-Handed (One-Handed if on a Mount), Unwieldy, Rare, Essence 25

Range: Reach 10 ft

APC: 6-STR (min 3)

Damage: 1d12+1 sharp

This steel lance has engravings of knights on horseback all along its shaft.

If you attack a target after moving at least 10 ft in their direction, you may apply a +3 bonus to your attack roll, or +5 if you are on horseback. You may target their AR or their FORT, and if you hit, in addition to taking damage, the creature also falls Prone. (3/Recoup)

DANCING SCIMITAR

DEX Weapon, One-Handed, Light, Rare, Essence 20

Range: Melee

APC: 5-DEX (min 3)

Damage: 1d6+2 sharp

This golden-hilted scimitar has a prominent red gem set in its hilt. It has one charge, which it regains at dawn. You may spend 2 AP and 1 charge to activate the Dancing Scimitar, turning the gem green. While activated, the Dancing Scimitar magically floats and attacks on its own, gaining its own turn immediately after yours. The Dancing Scimitar obeys your mental commands and may remain active for 1 minute or until it is reduced to 0 HP. While this feature is active, the Dancing Scimitar is no longer considered equipped for the purposes of attacks or abilities.

DUELIST’S DELIGHT

DEX Weapon, One-Handed, Ammunition, Light, Uncommon, Essence 15

Range: 60 ft

APC: 2

Damage: 1d4+1 sharp

This weapon appears to be a nondescript elm hand crossbow. This weapon has 2 charges and regains both charges at dawn each day. You may activate Duelist’s Delight by spending 1 charge when you roll Initiative. Doing so adds +5 to your Initiative and causes your first successful attack with the Duelist’s Delight within 1 minute of rolling Initiative to count as a crit.
