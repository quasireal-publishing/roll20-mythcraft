CHAPTER 7 | THE HOARD 

GUARDIAN’S SHIELD

Armor, Shield, Uncommon, Essence 15

Base: Heater Shield

This steel heater shield is edged in silver. You may spend 1 Reactive AP when an ally within 5 ft of you is attacked. Grant them +1 AR vs that attack. This bonus increases to +3 AR if used against a ranged weapon attack. You can use this feature once per round, resetting at the start of each of your turns.

ICEWALKER’S ARMOR

Armor, Medium, Uncommon, Essence 15

Base: Scale Mail

The icy white scales of this scale mail armor appear to be made of chitin. You gain Immune Cold.

IRON MAIDEN’S PLATE

Armor, Heavy, Unwieldy, Epic, Essence 30

Base: Plate (Full)

This suit of plate mail is made from dull, rusted iron and its interior is lined with blunted spikes. The helmet is molded into the visage of a screaming woman. You may activate the armor and immediately take 1d8 sharp damage. Until the start of your next turn, you gain +6 to attack and damage rolls with STR melee weapons and gain Immune Charmed, Demoralized, Enthralled, Frightened, and Shaken.

LEAFDANCER’S CUIRASS

Armor, Light, Uncommon, Essence 15

Base: Cuirass (Leather)

This cuirass is made of stitched green leaves. This armor has 1 charge, regaining its charge daily at dawn. You may expend the charge and spend 10 minutes in direct sunlight to regain 1d8 HP. Doing so also provides nourishment equivalent to a standard meal.

LUCKY CUTOFF VEST

Armor, Light, Common, Essence 10

Base: Gambeson

While wearing the Lucky Cutoff Vest, you can give yourself +5 to REF when your REF is attacked until the beginning of your next turn. This extra REF is free the first time you do it, and 2 Reactive AP on concurrent uses. You regain your free use upon Taking a Rest.

MIRROR SHIELD

Armor, Shield, Rare, Essence 25

Base: Heater Shield

This round shield has a perfectly mirrored silver surface. It has 3 charges, and regains 1 charge every dawn. You may spend 1 charge and 2 Reactive AP after being hit by a ranged attack that targets no other creatures. Any damage dealt from the attack is instead dealt back to the attacker. You can use this feature once per round, resetting at the start of each of your turns.

PHALANX TOWER SHIELD

Armor, Shield, Uncommon, Essence 25

Base: Tower Shield

This wooden tower shield is edged in bronze and has a single bronze cap at its center. The shield has 2 charges, and regains both charges at dawn each day. You may activate the Phalanx Tower Shield to create a 20 ft radius magical zone centered on where this feature was activated (it does not move with you). Creatures may pass freely into and out of the zone. Any creature inside the zone is immune to nonmagical ranged attacks that originate outside the zone. This zone lasts for 1 minute.

ROBES OF THE ARCHMAGE

Armor, Light, Epic, Essence 35

Base: Robes (Elemental)

This magnificently crafted black silk robe is fringed in gold thread and is stitched with the motif of a thousand stars. You gain Resist Elemental 8. Spells cost 2 fewer AP (min 1) for you to cast.

You may spend 6 AP to regain ½ your SP. (1/Rest)

SECOND SKIN

Armor, Light, Rare, Essence 20

Base: Hide Armor

This set of stitched hide armor appears to be made from various human skins. After being equipped, the armor molds with the wearer’s skin and cannot be removed except with Lesser Restoration or equivalent magic. While equipped, you gain Immune Petrified and Immune Paralyzed, and you cannot have your form changed – willingly or otherwise – by magic such as Polymorph.

SHIELD OF THORNS

Armor, Shield, Rare, Essence 20

Base: Buckler

This round iron buckler is covered in twisting metal thorns. You can make a 0 Reactive AP special attack immediately after a creature makes a melee attack against you. The special attack is +DEX vs REF. Hit: 1d6 sharp and the target gains Bleeding (1). You can use this feature once per round, resetting at the start of each of your turns.

SUNBURST SHIELD

Armor, Shield, Uncommon, Essence 15

Base: Heater Shield

This round wooden shield is painted with a highly detailed sun motif. The Sunburst Shield has 3 charges and regains 1 charge daily at dawn. You may spend 1 charge and 2 AP to activate the shield. Make a special attack: +CHA vs the WILL of all creatures in a 10 ft cone. Hit: Those creatures gain the Blinded condition until the end of their next turn.

SYMBIOTIC MIMIC

Armor, Light, Rare, Essence 20

Base: Leather Armor

This set of plain leather armor is actually a non-hostile mimic in disguise. You gain +2 AR and may spend 4 AP to change this armor’s base to leather armor, scale mail or plate (full). Additionally, you cannot be Surprised. However, when you regain HP, you only regain ½ that amount. Unequipping the armor requires a successful DC 14 CHA check; on a failure, you cannot attempt to remove the armor again for 24 hours. Lesser Restoration or similar magic can also remove the armor.

VOLCANIC PLATE

Armor, Heavy, Unwieldy, Epic, Essence 30

Base: Plate (Full)

This suit of field plate is made from pure obsidian and is covered in veins of faintly glowing red crystal. You gain Immune Fire but gain Vulnerable Cold 1. Additionally, you can activate the Volcanic Plate for 2 AP, which gives off an intense heat. This heat causes highly flammable objects that are not being worn or carried to catch fire. Any creature that starts its turn within 5 ft of you takes 1d6 fire damage while this feature is active. You may deactivate the armor for 2 AP.

ITEMS

AMULET OF THIRST

Necklace, Common, Essence 10

This simple amulet is made from a clear crystal wrapped in gold on a golden chain. The amulet has 1 charge and regains its charge at dawn each day. After spending 1 hour submerged in a potion, this amulet stores that potion’s effect as the crystal takes on that potion’s color. This expends the amulet’s charge. The potion then becomes mundane liquid. You may activate the amulet for 1 AP and immediately gain the stored effect as if you had consumed the potion yourself. After using this feature, the amulet’s crystal returns to its original clear state and no longer has any potion effects.

BOOK OF SECRETS

Book, Rare, Essence 35

This book is bound in an unidentifiable leather dyed a dark blue and is edged in elegant gold leaf. The book has 6 charges, and regains 1d4 charges at dawn each day. If examined, the pages inside are blank, and if you write in the book, it disappears after 1 hour. By spending 1 charge, speaking a command word, and saying the name of one humanoid within 10 miles of you, the pages within the book begin to fill with information about that target humanoid as if written by a personal biographer. Anyone who spends 30 minutes reading this book gains a +8 to any INT/History check made to learn information about the target humanoid, and gains a +6 to AWR/Intuiting, CHA/Intimidating, and CHA/Persuading when interacting with the target humanoid for the next 24 hours. This writing remains in the book for 1 hour, after which it fades and becomes blank once again.

BOOTS OF THE SWIFTWIND

Boots, Rare, Essence 20

These sheepskin boots are dyed a light grey-blue and have silver capped heels. These boots grant you +10 ft walking speed and double your horizontal jump distance. Once per day, you can click the heels of these boots together and gain a vertical jump height of 20 ft until the end of their next turn.

BOTTOMLESS BAG

Container, Uncommon, Essence 15

This green-velvet bag is tied with a golden string and can open to a diameter of 1 ft. The bag appears empty when examined or turned inside out but anything deposited into it disappears into a pocket dimension. By spending 2 AP, you can reach into the bag and retrieve something stored within this pocket dimension. It can be a random item (determined by the MC) or a specific one. The Bottomless Bag can hold up to 500 lbs of items but never weighs more than 2 lbs when worn. The bottomless bag’s pocket dimension contains no breathable air. The bag’s environment is considered ambient room temperature, and organic matter will rot at its normal rate.

BEASTHEART GAUNTLETS

Gloves, Rare, Essence 20

These leather gauntlets are wrapped in thick brown fur. You gain +1 STR and you gain an additional damage die to all your unarmed attacks (e.g. 1d4 becomes 2d4).
