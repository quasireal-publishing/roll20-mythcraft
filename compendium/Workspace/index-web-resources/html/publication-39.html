CHAPTER 7 | THE HOARD 

FROSTWALKER BOOTS

Boots, Rare, Essence 20

These leather boots are lined in snow-white fur. The boots have 1 charge, and regain 1 charge at midnight. You gain +6 to checks made to resist the effects of cold weather. You may spend 1 charge to speak a command word and activate the boots to gain the ability to walk on water for 10 minutes, as the water freezes solid beneath each step. At the end of this 10 minutes, any water frozen by the Frostwalker Boots immediately returns to its previous state.

GIRDLE OF THE WOLF

Worn Item, Rare, Essence 20

This simple belt is made from gray wolf fur. You gain +2 to melee attack rolls if you have an ally within 5 ft of your target. Additionally, you may spend 4 AP to transform into a Wolf. While transformed, you use your own HP, INT, and CHA, but otherwise use the stat block of a wolf with +2 to its DEX. As a wolf, you may spend your whole turn to transform back into your original form.

GLOVES OF THE THOUGHT THIEF

Gloves, Rare, Essence 30

These fingerless leather gloves have small milky white gems set over the knuckles. You gain +4 to INT/Sleight of Hand checks. Additionally, you may spend 2 AP to target a creature within 5 ft of you and make a special attack. Make a CHA attack against their WILL. On a hit, the target is Dazed until the end of their next turn and loses the memory of everything that happened in the last minute.

IVORY WHISTLE

Instrument, Rare, Essence 20

This small, hand-carved whistle appears to be made of ivory. When blown, a trail of smoke exits the whistle and takes the form of a black horse. This horse has the Draft Horse stat block. It appears in an unoccupied space within 20 ft of you, obeys your mental commands, and lasts for up to 24 hours, until dismissed for 1 AP, or until reaching 0 HP. Once this whistle has been activated, it cannot be activated again for three days.

MAD TINKERER’S TOOLBOX

Container, Rare, Essence 20

This plain-looking metal latched box has chipped red paint and plenty of dings and dents. It offers a +2 bonus to all Crafting checks made when you use this item. Once every 24 hours, when you would take damage from failing a survival or crafting check, you can choose to transfer any amount of that damage to the toolbox. This cannot reduce damage taken from creatures (such as bites, kicks, scratches, and the like). The toolbox is found with 10d8 (45) HP left. When the toolbox runs out of HP, any leftover damage from the transfer that caused it to break is directed back to you.

MAGNETIC GLOVES

Gloves, Uncommon, Essence 15

These leather gloves are interlaced with thin metal plates across the fingers and palms. You cannot be unwillingly disarmed of any metal object that you are holding and do not need to make STR checks when climbing a metal surface (you automatically succeed).

MASQUERADE MIRROR

Supernatural Item, Uncommon, Essence 15

This silver framed hand-mirror is wrapped in delicate engravings of leering masks. It has 2 charges and regains its charges at dusk each day. When you stare into the mirror for 30 minutes, you can expend the mirror’s charge to magically change your facial features, clothing, and physical appearance (but not your size) for 4 hours. This appearance can withstand casual scrutiny but is revealed to any spell or effect that sees through illusion magic.

MESSENGER BRACELET

Worn Item, Uncommon, Essence 15

This thin silver bracelet is inlaid with a clear oval stone. When two or more Messenger Bracelets are placed within 3 inches of one another for one hour the stones of all Messenger Bracelets shift to the same color. Until they are realigned with new bracelets, anyone wearing one of those Messenger Bracelets may whisper up to 25 words into their bracelet and have the message spoken directly into the minds of all others wearing those bracelets. The message is transferred in the language in which it was spoken. Each bracelet may use this feature once per hour.

RING OF PROTECTION

Ring, Rare, Essence 20

This golden ring is affixed with a single blue gemstone. You gain DR 2 against all damage.

SEA LEGS

Boots, Uncommon, Essence 15

These knee-high leather boots have a small buckle on each foot shaped like an anchor. You have Immune Shaken and Immune Sickened if you would suffer either condition as a result of physical disorientation. The boots have 1 charge, which it regains at dawn each day. You may utter a command word and expend 1 charge to become rooted to the spot where you are standing for 1 minute. While rooted, you cannot be moved willingly or unwillingly and are considered Grounded.

SPELL THIEF’S SCROLL

Scroll, Rare, Essence 20

This scroll is made from very fine parchment and bears a large circle of arcane sigils. While equipped, and if the Spell Thief’s Scroll is empty, you may spend 2 Reactive AP when you are hit by a spell that has no other targets. You cancel that spell’s effect and store the spell. You may cast that spell one time without spending SP; you cannot upcharge it. The spell is then removed from the scroll. Only one spell may be stored in the Spell Thief’s Scroll at a time.

VIPERSTRIKE BRACERS

Bracers, Epic, Essence 35

These iron bracers are decorated in carvings of striking vipers. You gain +2 AP per round. This additional AP may only be used for weapon attacks.

VISAGE OF FEAR

Helmet, Rare, Essence 20

This dark steel helmet includes a faceplate shaped like a snarling fiend. The item has 1 charge, which it regains at dusk each evening. While wearing this helmet, you suffer -1 CHA, but gain +6 to the Intimidating skill.

You may spend the item’s charge and 2 AP to make a CHA/Intimidating check vs a living target’s WILL. On a hit, the target gains the Phobic condition for 24 hours. You choose the phobia. It can be a specific creature, a type of animal or plant, a color, or a concept, such as the number seven. It cannot be something fundamental to the creature’s existence, such as breathing, eating, or their own face.

WANDERER’S DOORKNOCKER

Supernatural Item, Epic, Essence 40

This brass door knocker is shaped like a small angel clutching a large ring. You may spend 4 AP to attach the Wanderer’s Doorknocker to any door. By whispering a location that is familiar to you and on the same plane of existence, you may activate the Wanderer’s Doorknocker and cause the door to which it is attached to connect to a random door within 500 ft of the whispered location, determined by the MC. If no door is present, the Wanderer’s Doorknocker does not activate. This effect lasts for one minute, after which the Doorknocker falls to the ground at the whispered location’s doorstep, or until the Doorknocker is removed from the door by spending 1 AP. Once activated, the Wanderer’s Doorknocker cannot be used again for 24 hours.
