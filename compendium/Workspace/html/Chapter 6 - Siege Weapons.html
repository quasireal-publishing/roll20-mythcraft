Ballista

This ballista is bolted onto a ship's deck, a city wall, or another large area. Ballistae cost 4,000 sc.


Range: 3,000 ft

Ammunition: Bolt (1 AP)

Reload: 2 AP

AOE: None

Heft: 6

HP: 30

REF: 7

FORT: 14

Resist: Blunt 3

A creature within 5 ft of the Ballista can spend 3 AP to make a siege weapon attack vs AR. This attack suffers a -3 penalty if the target is Medium or smaller. Hit: 4d10 sharp.

BALLISTA (PORTABLE)

This ballista is mounted on a low-to-the-ground wagon. It can be saddled to horses or other beasts of burden, and any beast that can carry at least two unwieldy items can pull the ballista by themself. It is light enough to feasibly be dragged by a couple strong humanoids. Portable ballistae cost 1,500 sc.

BATTERING RAM

A battering ram is a thick tree trunk reinforced with metal and intended to break down gateways and doors. Battering rams cost 1,000 sc.


Range: 5 ft

Ammunition: -

Reload: -

AOE: None

Heft: 8

Speed: 40 ft

HP: 20

AR: 13

REF: 7

FORT: 13

Resist: Blunt 5

Immune: Psychic

DR: 5/Fire or Cold

DT: 10


A creature holding the battering ram may spend 3 AP to shove it forward and strike a surface. A creature can only make this attack if the battering ram's full Heft is met by the STR of all creatures holding the ram. This attack targets FORT and can only target inanimate objects. Hit: 6d6 blunt.

CANNON

A thick, heavy gun that relies on black powder to propel heavy iron balls at high speeds, a cannon is a devastating weapon that can tremendously turn the tide of a siege or naval conflict. A beast that can carry three or more unwieldy items can drag the cannon by itself when the cannon is mounted on wheels. Cannons cost 2,200 sc.


Range: 2,500 ft

Ammunition: Ball (2 AP)

Reload: 2 AP

AOE: 10 ft radius

Heft: 8

Speed: 30 ft

HP: 60

AR: 15

REF: 8

FORT: 14

Resist: Blunt 5

Immune: Psychic

DR: 5/Cold

DT: 13


A creature within 5 ft of the Cannon can spend 3 AP to make a siege weapon attack vs the FORT of each target in the radius. This attack suffers a -2 penalty against Medium or smaller targets. Hit: 6d10 blunt and the target falls Prone. Miss: ½ damage and the target does not fall Prone. If the cannonball did not impact a surface (such as targeting a flying creature), it does not use its AOE radius and does no damage on a miss.

CATAPULT

Catapults are heavy contraptions that can launch their payloads at high speeds and arching trajectories. While not as powerful as trebuchets, catapults are more maneuverable and can be used to launch specialty ammunition over a fortified wall. Beasts that can carry five or more unwieldy items can drag a catapult if it is mounted on wheels. A team of beasts that can collectively carry that many unwieldy items can also move the catapult. Catapults cost 5,000 sc.

SIEGE TOWER

Siege towers are enormous, wheeled towers usually made of wood and reinforced with iron. They provide total cover to creatures within the tower and have drawbridges that can be lowered to allow soldiers to run from the top of the siege tower directly onto an enemy's battlements. Beasts that can carry seven or more unwieldy items can drag or push a siege tower. A team of beasts that can collectively carry that many unwieldy items can also move the tower. Siege towers cost 8,000 sc.


Heft: 20

Speed: 20 ft

HP: 200

AR: 14

REF: 5

FORT: 16

Resist: Blunt 6

Immune: Psychic

Vulnerable: Fire 1

DR: 2/Fire

DT: 15


A creature in the top floor of the siege tower may spend 3 AP to cut the ropes, causing the drawbridge to rapidly lower.

TREBUCHET

A much larger and more powerful cousin to the catapult, a trebuchet is dozens of feet tall and can hurl stone balls almost as far as a cannon. Beasts that can carry seven or more unwieldy items can drag a trebuchet if it is mounted on wheels. A team of beasts that can collectively carry that many unwieldy items can also move the trebuchet. Trebuchets cost 9,000 sc.
